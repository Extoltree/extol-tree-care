Tree & health care

About Extol Tree Care

Extol Tree care was founded in 2022 by Joseph and Melissa Beachy to provide high quality tree care. After more than a decade of working in multiple different industries, Joseph began work as an arborist with the goal of starting his own company. Joseph has always loved being in the outdoors and creating beauty in nature. Joseph fell in love with climbing trees at a young age and loves that he can now do it for a living.

Extol means to glorify and highly praise, especially in reference to God. We chose this name because it reflects the heart of our work and our lives. Our work is rooted in the belief that creating and cultivating beauty in outdoor spaces is an expression of stewardship, creativity, and gratitude. As we cultivate beauty, we seek to honor and glorify God through the work of our hands.
