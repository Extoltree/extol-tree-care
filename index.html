Client Services

We provide a wide range of arboricultural services, following ISA (International Society of Arboriculture) and TCIA (Tree Care Industry Association) standards, and best practices.

  • Structural Pruning (young to mature trees)
  • Tree removal (small to large trees)
  • Cabling and bracing
  • Root collar investigation and remediation
  • Root invigoration
  • Hazardous tree and limb removals
  • Deadwood removal
  • Getting “just that one branch”
  • Rescuing cats, kites, drones, and more

About Extol Tree Care

Extol Tree care was founded in 2022 by Joseph and Melissa Beachy to provide high quality tree care. After more than a decade of working in multiple different industries, Joseph began work as an arborist with the goal of starting his own company. Joseph has always loved being in the outdoors and creating beauty in nature. Joseph fell in love with climbing trees at a young age and loves that he can now do it for a living.

Extol means to glorify and highly praise, typically in reference to God. We chose that name because it describes our goal for the company and our lives. When we create, foster, and enjoy beauty in outdoor spaces, it accomplishes what we are designed to do: glorify God.
